1.Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Bookaholic collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Bookaholic's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Bookaholic's visitors use its website. From time to time, Bookaholic may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Bookaholic also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on http://www.bookaholic.com blog posts. Bookaholic only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

The amount and type of information that Bookaholic gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who leave a comment at http://www.bookaholic.com to provide a username and email address.

Bookaholic uses the remarketing services to advertise on third party websites (including Google) to previous visitors to our site. 9.Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Bookaholic uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Bookaholic uses cookies to help Bookaholic identify and track visitors, their usage of http://www.bookaholic.com, and their website access preferences. Bookaholic visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Bookaholic's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Bookaholic's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Bookaholic's use of cookies.
